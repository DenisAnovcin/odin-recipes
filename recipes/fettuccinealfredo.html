Fettuccine Alfredo, an italian delicacy that will fill you with the creamy and rich taste of parmeggiano and butter!

Fettuccine Alfredo is a luxurious and comforting Italian pasta dish characterized by its rich, creamy sauce. The recipe begins with cooking fettuccine pasta until it reaches a perfect al dente texture. Meanwhile, a velvety Alfredo sauce is prepared by melting butter and combining it with heavy cream and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. The sauce is seasoned with a touch of garlic, salt, and pepper, enhancing its savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 250g Fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup Parmeggiano Regiano (24 months)
  • 200g Butter
  • salt an pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the packaging information.
  2. Cut butter into small pieces and put it on a plate.
  3. Prepare the cheese by shredding it.
  4. Transfer finished pasta onto the plate.
  5. Keep mixing the pasta with butter until completely dissolved.
  6. Transfer the cheese onto the plate and mixed until immulsified.
  7. Enjoy!
