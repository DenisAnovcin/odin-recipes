Aglio e Olio, an italian delicacy that will fill you with delicious garlic!

Aglio e Olio is a classic Italian pasta dish that originated in Naples. It features a simple combination of garlic (aglio), olive oil (olio), red pepper flakes, and pasta, typically spaghetti. The garlic is gently saut√©ed in olive oil until fragrant, then tossed with the cooked pasta and red pepper flakes for a bit of heat. It's often finished with a sprinkle of parsley and grated Parmesan, though the latter is optional. The dish is known for its bold, savory flavors and is a quick and easy favorite among pasta lovers.

Ingredients

  • 250g spaghetti pasta
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • chilli flakes/peperoncino (optional)
  • salt an pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the packaging information.
  2. On a pan, heat up just enough olive oil to cover the surface.
  3. In the meantime, cut garlic cloves into small round pieces.
  4. Fry the garlic untill golden, add peperoncino if you are using it.
  5. Transfer finished pasta, with a bit of the pasta water, into the pan.
  6. Mix thoroughly to emulsify oil with pasta water.
  7. Take out the pasta from the pan and serve.
  8. Enjoy!
